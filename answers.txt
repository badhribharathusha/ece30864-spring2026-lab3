(a) What are some Pros and Cons of Agile development (vs non-agile)? List 2 of each.
    With agile development, two Pro's you have include faster continuous feedback and high adaptability to change. Two Con's inlcude high client involvement and unpredictable timelines.

(b) Explain the role of the Scrum Master vs the Product Owner. The product owner focuses more on the vision of the product, focusing on what the stakeholders want and things like that. The Scrum Master guides the team through the development process and leads the actual development process.

(c) What are 3 types of meetings that happen during a sprint cycle? List at least 2 points on what needs to be discussed during each of these meetings.
    Sprint planning is where you define sprint goals and select backlog items and this takes place in the beginning of the sprint. The Daily Standup is a daily meeting where you discuss what each team member does for the day and their plans for development and change up items if needed. Finally the sprint review is at the end of the sprint cycle where you display your developed items and you get feedback on it. 
    
(d) Differentiate between Epic, Story, Bug, and Task issue types in JIRA
Epics are a large body of work that contain a lot of other things within and can last multiple sprints. A story describes a specific feature usually from an end-user perspective and is targeted to be finished within a sprint. A task is a to-do for technical work necessary for development. Finally a bug is issue in functionality that needs fixing.
